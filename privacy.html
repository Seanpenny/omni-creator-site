Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Omni Creator Engine is a creator workflow tool used to prepare content, scripts, media files, captions, and publishing packages for accounts controlled by the user.

Information We Process

Users may provide account names, email addresses, topics, scripts, images, videos, captions, and platform connection details required to prepare or publish content.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to generate and organize content workflows, prepare exports, connect approved platform APIs, and help users manage their own creator accounts.

Sharing

Omni does not sell personal information. Content or account data is shared with third-party platforms only when the user chooses to connect or publish through those services.

User Control

Users control their own platform accounts and may disconnect platform access or request deletion of stored workflow data from their own deployment.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the site owner through the associated creator or business account.
